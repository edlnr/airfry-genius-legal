Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

AirFry Genius: Cooking Times ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Personal Information: Name (for personalization)
  • Air Fryer Information: Brand, model, wattage, and basket size (to provide accurate cooking recommendations)
  • Cooking Preferences: Measurement units (metric/imperial), language preference
  • Recipe Data: Ingredients, cooking times, temperatures, and notes you save
  • Apple User ID: If you choose to sign in with Apple (optional, used for account sync across devices)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: We use Amplitude to collect anonymous usage data including:
    • App screens visited and features used
    • Cooking sessions and recipe interactions
    • Error logs and crash reports
    • Session duration and frequency
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, language settings
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status through RevenueCat (we do not store payment details)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Personalized Cooking Recommendations: Your air fryer details and preferences help us calculate accurate cooking times and temperatures
  • Save and Sync Recipes: Store your saved recipes and sync them across your devices (if signed in with Apple)
  • Improve the App: Analytics help us understand how users interact with the app and identify areas for improvement
  • Customer Support: Troubleshoot issues and respond to your requests
  • Subscription Management: Process and manage your subscription through Apple's In-App Purchase system
We do NOT:
  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Share your recipe data with anyone
  • Track you across other apps or websites

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Amplitude Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics and app improvement. Data is stored in the EU region. Amplitude Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: For subscription and payment processing. RevenueCat does not store payment information. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In: Optional authentication service. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Workers: Backend infrastructure for cooking recommendations. Cloudflare Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your recipes and preferences are primarily stored locally on your device using secure storage
  • Cloud Sync: If you sign in with Apple, your data is synced securely to our backend
  • Security Measures: We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) to protect data in transit
  • Data Retention: Your data is retained until you delete your account or uninstall the app

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: View all your data stored in the app (Settings > Account)
  • Modify: Update your name, air fryer details, and preferences at any time
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data (Settings > Account > Delete Account)
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You can limit ad tracking in your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Tracking)
  • Export: Your recipe data is visible in the app; you can manually export by viewing individual recipes

6. Children's Privacy

AirFry Genius is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We use Amplitude's EU data residency to minimize international transfers. When transfers occur, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, you can:

  • Delete your account directly in the app (Settings > Account > Delete Account)
  • Contact Apple Support if you have issues with your Apple Sign In

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information (available in app)
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Legal basis for processing: Consent (onboarding), Contract (subscription), Legitimate interests (app improvement)
